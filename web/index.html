How is this calculated?

Human baseline: 4 min × $75/hr loaded rate = $5.00 per issue. The engineer reads the issue, picks a label, sets priority.

With LLM: wrong predictions (1 − accuracy) still need full human review. Correct predictions only need a 20% spot-check at 1 min each.

Formula: saved = (issues × 4 min) − (wrong × 4 min + right × 20% × 1 min)

ROI multiplier: human cost per issue ÷ LLM API cost per issue. Volume-independent — works for any repo size.

Break-even: net savings turn positive when monthly volume is high enough that saved engineer time exceeds API cost × 12.
